Laws of the Phospharo Republic

Current status: Active Laws of the Republic
Article 1 (The State)

The constitutional name of this nation shall be the Phospharo Republic, and it shall be commonly known as the Republic of Luminara.

Article 2 (National Territory)

This digital space is officially recognized as the sovereign territory and home of its citizens.
